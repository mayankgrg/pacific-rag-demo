Acme Corp — Q4 2024 Financial Summary (Restricted — Finance and Leadership Only)

This document contains material non-public financial information. Unauthorized distribution is a violation of company policy and may have legal consequences.

Revenue

Total Q4 2024 revenue: $4.2M
- Subscription revenue: $3.6M (86% of total)
- Professional services revenue: $0.6M (14% of total)

Full-year 2024 revenue: $14.8M
Year-over-year growth: +38% (vs. $10.7M in FY2023)

ARR as of December 31, 2024: $15.2M
- Net new ARR added in Q4: $1.1M
- Churned ARR in Q4: $180K
- Net Revenue Retention (NRR): 118%

ARR by customer segment:
- Enterprise (>$100K ACV): $8.4M (55% of ARR)
- Mid-Market ($20K–$100K ACV): $5.6M (37% of ARR)
- SMB (<$20K ACV): $1.2M (8% of ARR)

Expenses

Total Q4 2024 operating expenses: $3.8M
Monthly burn rate: $1.27M (Q4 average)

Breakdown:
- R&D (Engineering): $1.45M (38%)
- Sales & Marketing: $1.14M (30%)
- G&A (HR, Finance, Legal, Ops): $0.76M (20%)
- Customer Success: $0.45M (12%)

Headcount-related costs (salaries + benefits + equity): $3.1M of total OpEx
Non-headcount OpEx (AWS, tools, office, travel): $0.7M

Operating Income / Loss

Q4 2024 operating loss: -$620K
Q4 2024 operating margin: -14.8%

This is an improvement from Q3 2024 operating loss of -$890K (-24.6% margin), driven by revenue growth outpacing expense growth.

Cash Position

Cash and cash equivalents as of December 31, 2024: $11.4M
Runway at current burn rate: approximately 9 months

Note: The company is actively in Series B fundraising discussions. A term sheet is expected by end of Q1 2025. Details are strictly confidential.

Q1 2025 Projections

Projected Q1 2025 revenue: $4.7M–$5.0M
Projected Q1 2025 ARR at end of quarter: $16.5M–$17.0M
Projected operating loss: -$500K to -$700K (improvement driven by sales productivity gains and headcount stabilization)

Key Metrics

Customer count: 418 (up from 376 at end of Q3 2024)
Average ACV: $36,400
Gross margin: 74% (up from 71% in Q3 2024)
CAC payback period: 14 months
LTV:CAC ratio: 4.2x

Board and Investor Notes

This summary was presented to the board on January 15, 2025. The board approved the Q1 2025 operating plan, including the hiring freeze on non-revenue-generating roles and the acceleration of the Series B process. The next board meeting is scheduled for April 10, 2025.
